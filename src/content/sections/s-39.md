<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Whoever, not being authorised by the Authority, uses or tampers with the data in the Central Identities Data Repository or in any removable storage medium with the intent of modifying information relating to Aadhaar number holder or discovering any information thereof, shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to <span class="amend" data-id="p20.2" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-target="“three years“" data-note="“three years“ substituted by Act 14 of 2019">ten years</span> and shall also be liable to a fine which may extend to ten thousand rupees.</p>
</div>
