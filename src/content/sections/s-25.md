<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) There shall be constituted a Fund to be called the Unique Identification Authority of India Fund and there shall be credited thereto—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) all grants, fees and charges received by the Authority under this Act; and</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) all sums received by the Authority from such other sources as may be decided upon by the Central Government.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Fund shall be applied for meeting—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) the salaries and allowances payable to the Chairperson and members and administrative expenses including the salaries, allowances and pension payable to or in respect of officers and other employees of the Authority; and</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the expenses on objects and for purposes authorised by this Act.</p></div>
