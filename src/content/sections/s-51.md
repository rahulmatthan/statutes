<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The Authority may, by general or special order in writing, delegate to any <span class="amend" data-id="p24.2" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-target="“Member, officer“" data-note="“member, officer“ substituted by Act 14 of 2019">Member or officer</span> of the Authority or any other person, subject to such conditions, if any, as may be specified in the order, such of its powers and functions under this Act (except the power under section 54) as it may deem necessary.</p>
