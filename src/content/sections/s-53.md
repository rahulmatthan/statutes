<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Central Government may, by notification, make rules to carry out the provisions of this Act.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In particular, and without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing power, such rules may provide for all or any of the following matters, namely:—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) the form and manner in which and the authority before whom the oath of office and of secrecy is to be subscribed by the Chairperson and Members under sub-section (2) of section 14;</p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p24.3" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(aa) the purpose for which the requesting entity may be allowed by the Authority to perform authentication under sub-clause (ii) of clause (b) of sub-section (4) of section 4;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the salary and allowances payable to, and other terms and conditions of service of, the Chairperson and the allowances or remuneration payable to Members of the Authority under sub-section (4) of section 14;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) the other powers and functions of the Chairperson of the Authority under section 17;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) the other powers and functions of the Authority under clause (t) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) the form of annual statement of accounts to be prepared by Authority under sub-section (1) of section 26;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(f) the form and the manner in which and the time within which returns and statements and particulars are to be furnished under sub-section (1) of section 27;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(g) the form and the manner and the time at which the Authority shall furnish annual report under sub-section (2) of section 27;</p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.1" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(ga) the qualification and experience of, and the manner of appointment of, the Adjudicating Officer under sub-section (1) of section 33B;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.1" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(gb) the form, manner, and fee for an appeal to be filed under sub-section (2) of section 33C;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause">(h) any other matter which is required to be, or may be, prescribed, or in respect of which provision is to be or may be made by rules.</p>
</div>
