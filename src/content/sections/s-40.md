Penalty for unauthorised use by requesting entity.—Whoever, being a requesting entity, uses the identity information of an individual in contravention of sub-section (3) of section 8, shall be punishable with imprisonment which may extend to three years or with a fine which may extend to ten thousand rupees or, in the case of a company, with a fine which may extend to one lakh rupees or with both."

<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Whoever,—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) being a requesting entity, uses the identity information of an individual in contravention of sub-section (2) of section 8; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) being an offline verification-seeking entity, uses the identity information of an individual in contravention of sub-section (2) of section 8A, shall be punishable with imprisonment which may extend to three years or with a fine which may extend to ten thousand rupees or, in the case of a company, with a fine which may extend to one lakh rupees or with both.</p></div>
