<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Authority shall maintain authentication records in such manner and for such period as may be specified by regulations.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Every Aadhaar number holder shall be entitled to obtain his authentication record in such manner as may be specified by regulations.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The Authority shall not, either by itself or through any entity under its control, collect, keep or maintain any information about the purpose of authentication.</p>
</div>
