<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No act or proceeding of the Authority shall be invalid merely by reason of—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) any vacancy in, or any defect in the constitution of, the Authority;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) any defect in the appointment of a person as Chairperson or Member of the Authority; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) any irregularity in the procedure of the Authority not affecting the merits of the case.</p>
</div>
