<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Authority may, by notification, make regulations consistent with this Act and the rules made thereunder, for carrying out the provisions of this Act.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In particular, and without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing power, such regulations may provide for all or any of the following matters, namely:—</p>

  <p class="block clause">(b) the manner of verifying the demographic information and biometric information for issue of Aadhaar number under sub-section (3) of section 3;</p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.3" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(ba) the manner of generating an alternative virtual identity under sub- section (4) of section 3;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.3" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(bb) the manner in which cancellation of an Aadhaar number may be carried out under sub-section (2) of section 3A;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) the conditions for accepting an Aadhaar number as proof of identity of the Aadhaar number holder under sub-section (3) of section 4;</p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.4" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(ca) standards of privacy and security to be complied with by the requesting entities under sub-section (4) of section 4;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p25.4" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(cb) the classification of requesting entities under sub-section (5) of section 4;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) the other categories of individuals under section 5 for whom the Authority shall take special measures for allotment of Aadhaar number;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) the manner of updating biometric information and demographic information under section 6;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(f) the procedure for authentication of the Aadhaar number under section 8;</p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p26.1" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(fa) the alternate and viable means of identification of individual under the proviso to clause (b) of sub-section (2) of section 8;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p26.1" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">(fb) the manner of obtaining consent under clause (a) of sub-section (2), the manner of providing information to the individual undergoing offline verification under sub-section (3), and the obligations of offline verification-seeking entities under clause (c) of sub-section (4), of section 8A;</span></p>
  <p class="block clause">(g) the other functions to be performed by the Central Identities Data Repository under section 10;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(h) the time and places of meetings of the Authority and the procedure for transaction of business to be followed by it, including the quorum, under sub-section (1) of section 19;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) the salary and allowances payable to, and other terms and conditions of service of, the chief executive officer, officers and other employees of the Authority under sub-section (2) of section 21;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(j) the demographic information and biometric information under clause (a) and the manner of their collection under clause (b) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(k) the manner of maintaining and updating the information of individuals in the Central Identities Data Repository under clause (f) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(l) the manner of omitting and deactivating an Aadhaar number and information relating thereto under clause (g) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(m) the manner of use of Aadhaar numbers for the purposes of providing or availing of various subsidies, benefits, services and other purposes for which Aadhaar numbers may be used under clause (h) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(n) the terms and conditions for appointment of Registrars, enrolling agencies and other service providers and the revocation of appointments thereof under clause (i) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(o) the manner of sharing information of Aadhaar number holder under clause (k) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>

  <p class="block clause">(r) manner of authorising Registrars, enrolling agencies or other service providers to collect such fees for services provided by them under clause (o) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(s) policies and practices to be followed by the Registrar, enrolling agencies and other service providers under clause (r) of sub-section (2) of section 23;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(t) the manner of accessing the identity information by the Aadhaar number holder under the proviso to sub-section (5) of section 28;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(u) the manner of sharing the identity information, other than core biometric information, collected or created under this Act under sub- section (2) of section 29;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(v) the manner of alteration of demographic information under sub-section (1) and biometric information under sub-section (2) of section 31;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(w) the manner of and the time for maintaining the request for authentication and the response thereon under sub-section (1), and the manner of obtaining, by the Aadhaar number holder, the authentication records under sub-section (2) of section 32;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(x) any other matter which is required to be, or may be, specified, or in respect of which provision is to be or may be made by regulations.</p>
