<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) No court shall take cognizance of any offence punishable under this Act, save on a complaint made by the Authority or any officer or person authorised by it:</p>
  <p class="block proviso"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="p22.1" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2019-amendment-act" data-by-text="Act 14 of 2019" data-note="Inserted by Act 14 of 2019">Provided that the court may, on a complaint made by an Aadhaar number holder or individual take cognizance of any offence punishable under section 34 or 35 or 36 or 37 or 40 or section 41.</span></p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) No court inferior to that of a Chief Metropolitan Magistrate or a Chief Judicial Magistrate shall try any offence punishable under this Act.</p>
