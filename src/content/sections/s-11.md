<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Central Government shall, by notification, establish an Authority to be known as the Unique Identification Authority of India to be responsible for the processes of enrolment and authentication and perform such other functions assigned to it under this Act.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Authority shall be a body corporate by the name aforesaid, having perpetual succession and a common seal, with power, subject to the provisions of this Act, to acquire, hold and dispose of property, both movable and immovable, and to contract, and shall, by the said name, sue or be sued.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The head office of the Authority shall be in New Delhi.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(4) The Authority may, with the prior approval of the Central Government, establish its offices at other places in India.</p>
