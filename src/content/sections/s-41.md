<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Whoever, being an enrolling agency or a requesting entity, fails to comply with the requirements of sub-section (2) of section 3 or sub-section (3) of section 8, shall be <span class="amend" data-id="p21.2" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2019-07-25" data-by="2023-jan-vishwas" data-by-text="The Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Act, 2023 (18 of 2023)">liable to penalty which may extend to one lakh rupees, or in the case of a company, with penalty which may extend to ten lakh rupees</span>.</p>
