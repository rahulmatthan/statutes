The Ordinance was promulgated on 2 March 2019 to give effect to the post-*Puttaswamy* amendments to the Aadhaar Act, 2016 while Parliament was not in session. It was replaced — with the same substantive content — by the [Aadhaar and Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2019](/amendments/2019-amendment-act) on 25 July 2019.

For the per-section change list, see the History block of each affected section.
