<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires, -</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) “Act” means the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016 (18 of 2016);</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) “Form” means Form annexed to these rules;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) “Schedule” means the Schedule annexed to these rules;</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Words and expressions used and not defined in these rules, but defined in the Act shall have the meanings respectively assigned to them in the Act.</p>
