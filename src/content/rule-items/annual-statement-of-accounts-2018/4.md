<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The Authority shall preserve the Balance-Sheet, the Income and Expenditure Account and the Receipt and Payment Account referred to in sub-rule (1) of rule 3, for a period of five years following the financial year to which they relate.</p>
