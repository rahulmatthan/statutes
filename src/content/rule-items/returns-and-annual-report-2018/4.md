<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The A uthority shall prepare, as soon as may be, after the completion of each financial year the annual report referred to in sub-section (2) of section 27 of the Act in the form specified in Appendix-II.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Authority shall prepare once in every year an annual report giving an account of its activities performed in the previous year, containing the following, namely–</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) a description of all the activities of the Authority for the previous year;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the annual accounts for the previous year;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) the programmes of work for coming year.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The annual report shall after adoption at a meeting of the Authority, be signed by the members and authenticated by the Authority and the requisite number of copies thereof shall be submitted to the Central Government by the 31th day of December and the Central Government shall cause the same to be laid before each House of Parliament.</p>
</div>
