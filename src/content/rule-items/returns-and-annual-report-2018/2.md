<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In these rules unless the context otherwise requires, –</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) “Act” means the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) “annual report” means the annual report prepared by the Authority under sub-section (2) of section 27 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) “Appendix” means an Appendix annexed to these rules;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) “section” means a section of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) “year” means the financial year beginning the 1st day of April and ending on the 31st day of March of the year immediately following;</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) All other words and expressions used herein and not defined but defined in the Act shall have the same meanings assigned to them in the Act.</p>
</div>
