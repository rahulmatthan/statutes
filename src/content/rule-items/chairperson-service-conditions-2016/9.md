<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The full time Chairperson shall be entitled to thirty days of earned leave for every year of service.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The payment of leave salary during leave shall be governed by rule 40 of the Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The full-time Chairperson shall be entitled to encashment of fifty per-cent. of earned leave standing to their credit at any time, subject to the condition that the encashment shall be permitted only once during any calendar year for a maximum of twelve days in a calendar year.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(4) The President of India shall be the competent authority to sanction leave to the full-time Chairperson.</p>
</div>
