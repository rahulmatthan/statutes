<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Chairperson of the Authority and every part-time Member thereof shall be appointed by the Central Government on the recommendation of a Search-cum-Selection Committee consisting of —</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) Cabinet Secretary - Chairman ex officio;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) Secretary, Department of Personnel and Training - Member ex officio;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) Finance Secretary, Ministry of Finance - Member ex officio;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) Secretary, Department of Electronics and Information Technology - Member ex officio;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) two experts from the relevant field to be nominated by the Minister in-charge of the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Search-cum-Selection Committee may regulate its own procedure for the purpose of sub-rule (1).</p>
</div>
