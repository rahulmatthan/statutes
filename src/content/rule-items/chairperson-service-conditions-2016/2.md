<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires, -</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) “Act” means the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016 (18 of 2016);</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b)“Authority” means the Unique Identification Authority of India established under sub-section (1) of section 11 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) “full-time Chairperson” means the Chairperson of the Authority appointed on full time basis under section 12 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d)“Member” means the part-time Member of the Authority appointed under section 12 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e)“part-time Chairperson” means the Chairperson of the Authority appointed on part-time basis under section 12 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Words and expressions used herein but not defined, and defined in the Act, shall have the same meanings assigned to them in the Act.</p>
</div>
