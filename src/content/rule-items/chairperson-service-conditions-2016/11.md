<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Chairperson shall not be entitled to gratuity.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The full-time Chairperson shall be eligible to join the National Pension System applicable to the Central Government employees.</p>
</div>
