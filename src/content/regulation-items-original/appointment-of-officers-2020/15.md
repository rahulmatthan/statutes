title: "Power to relax"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Where the Authority is of the opinion that it is necessary or expedient so to do, it may, by order, and for reasons to be recorded in writing, relax any of the provisions of these regulations with respect to any class or category of persons.</p>
</div>
