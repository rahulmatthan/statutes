title: "Discharge of duties of higher grade and lower grade in certain cases"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The Competent Authority may, by order, having regard to the exigency of service, direct, -</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) to look after work of next higher grade for such period as may be specified in the order, to the officer or employee while he continues to hold a particular post and responsibility without any pecuniary benefit; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) an officer or employee, belonging to a higher grade in the cadre while holding such post, to look after the responsibilities relating to a post in the immediately lower grade without any pecuniary or other loss. Chapter III Miscellaneous</p>
</div>
