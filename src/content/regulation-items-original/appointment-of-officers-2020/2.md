<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In these regulations unless the context otherwise requires, ‒</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) “Act” means the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016 (18 of 2016), as amended vide the Aadhaar and Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2019 (14 of 2019);</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) “Appointing Authority” means Chief Executive Officer or such other officer to whom such powers and functions have been delegated by the Authority by general or special order;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) “Authority” means Unique Identification Authority of India established under sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) “Chairperson” means the Chairperson of the Authority appointed on full-time or part-time basis under Section 12 of the Act;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) “Chief Executive Officer” means Chief Executive Officer of the Authority appointed under Section 18 of the Act by the Central Government;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(f) “Post” means any post in a grade whether permanent or temporary mentioned in the Schedule annexed to these regulations;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(g) “Selection Committee” means a committee constituted to consider selection for appointment, absorption, promotion and deputation of officers and other employees in any grade in accordance with the Schedule to these regulations.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The words and expressions used in these regulations, but not defined, shall have the same meaning as respectively assigned to them under the Act.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) For the purposes of these regulations, the terms ‘he’, ‘him’, ‘his’ and ‘himself shall also refer to ‘she', ‘her’ and ‘herself’ wherever the context warrants and singular will also include plural.</p>
</div>
