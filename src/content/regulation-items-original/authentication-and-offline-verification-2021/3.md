title: "Types of Authentication Facilities"
chapter: "II — Aadhaar Authentication Framework"
as_of: 2021-11-08
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">There shall be two types of authentication facilities provided by the Authority, namely—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Yes/No authentication facility, which may be carried out using any of the modes specified in regulation 4(2); and</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) e-KYC authentication facility, which may be carried out only using OTP and/ or biometric authentication modes as specified in regulation 4(2).</p>
</div>
