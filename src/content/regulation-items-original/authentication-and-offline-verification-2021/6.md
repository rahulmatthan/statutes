title: "Consent of the Aadhaar number holder"
chapter: "II — Aadhaar Authentication Framework"
as_of: 2021-11-08
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) After communicating the information in accordance with Regulation 5, a requesting entity or Offline Verification Seeking Entity (OVSE) shall obtain the consent of the Aadhaar number holder or in case of a child, the consent of the parent or guardian of the child for the authentication or verification.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) A requesting entity or OVSE shall obtain the consent referred to in sub-regulation (1) above in physical or preferably in electronic form and maintain logs or records of the consent obtained in the manner and form as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose. 7. Capturing of biometric information by requesting entity—</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(1) A requesting entity shall capture the biometric information of the Aadhaar number holder using certified biometric devices as per the processes and specifications laid down by the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(1a) All biometric devices used for authentication shall be Registered Devices as per the standards specified by the Authority from time to time.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(1b) All the biometric devices shall be registered with the server of the requesting entity.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) A requesting entity shall necessarily encrypt and secure the biometric data at the time of capture as per the specifications laid down by the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) For optimum results in capturing of biometric information, a requesting entity shall adopt the processes as may be specified by the Authority from time to time for this purpose.</p>
</div>
