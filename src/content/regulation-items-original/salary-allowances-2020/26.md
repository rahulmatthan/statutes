title: "Loans and advances"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The Central Government’s rules and orders on interest free and interest bearing loans and advances applicable to the employees of the Central Government, as modified or substituted from time to time, shall, mutatis mutandis, apply to the employees of the Authority.</p>
</div>
