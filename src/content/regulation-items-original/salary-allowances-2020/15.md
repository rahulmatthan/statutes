number: "15"
title: "Medical facility"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Employees of the Authority shall be entitled to medical facilities as specified in the Second Schedule annexed to these regulations.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Nothing in these regulations shall prevent employees of the Authority to avail medical facilities available under the Central Government Health Scheme for the class of employees eligible under the Scheme.</p>
</div>
