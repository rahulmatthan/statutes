parent: salary-allowances-2020
number: "39"
title: "Seeking to influence"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall bring or attempt to bring any political or other outside influence to bear upon any superior authority to further his interests in respect of matters pertaining to his services in the Authority.</p>
</div>
