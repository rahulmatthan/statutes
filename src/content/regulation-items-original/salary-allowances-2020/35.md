number: "35"
title: "Employee not to seek outside employment"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall accept, solicit or seek any outside employment or office, whether stipendiary or honorary, without the prior sanction of the Competent Authority.</p>
</div>
