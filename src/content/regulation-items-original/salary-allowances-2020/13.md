title: "Re-fixation of pay on promotion and on confirmation"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Pay of an employee promoted from one grade to another shall be fixed in such a manner as admissible to the employees of the Central Government under corresponding grade.</p>
</div>
