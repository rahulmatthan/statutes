title: "Common proceedings"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Where two or more employees are concerned in a case, the Disciplinary Authority may make an order directing that the disciplinary proceedings against all of them may be taken in common proceedings.</p>
</div>
