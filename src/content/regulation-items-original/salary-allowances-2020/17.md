title: "Group insurance"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The employees of the Authority, other than those on deputation, shall be entitled to Group Insurance.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The scheme for Group Insurance shall be formulated by the Authority.</p>
</div>
