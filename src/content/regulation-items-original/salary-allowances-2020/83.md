title: "Property of the Authority"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Any material such as documents or information or reports generated by an employee or those received or obtained by an employee during the course of his official duty, from internal or external sources, shall be property of the Authority.</p>
</div>
