number: "45"
title: "Speculation in stocks, shares, investments, etc"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) An employee shall not indulge in badla trading or trade, speculate in stock, shares, securities or commodities of any description.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) No employee, shall, when in knowledge of unpublished price sensitive information, encourage any person to deal in the securities to which it relates.</p>
</div>
