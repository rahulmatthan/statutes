title: "Travel on official tours, transfer etc"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) An employee of the Authority shall be liable to proceed on tour for official duty to any place within India or abroad as and when so required by the भाग III खण्ड भारत का रािहत्र : असाधारण 33 Authority for which he may be paid Travelling Allowance, Daily Allowance and such other allowances as admissible to the employees of the Central Government of corresponding grade.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) For the purpose of regulating the claims of the employees of the Authority related to travel on official tours including foreign tours, transfer etc., the provisions of the rules applicable to the central government employees of the corresponding grade and orders thereunder, shall apply, mutatis mutandis, to the employees of the Authority.</p>
</div>
