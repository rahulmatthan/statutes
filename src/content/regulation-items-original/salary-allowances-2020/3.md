title: "Decision by the Competent Authority and appeal thereof"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The powers exercisable by the Competent Authority under these regulations shall also be exercisable by any authority superior to the Competent Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Chief Executive Officer or the officer to whom such powers have been vested under sub- regulation (1) shall be responsible for deciding on all complaints, grievances, disputes and other similar matters arising under these regulations:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that any employee aggrieved of such decision may make an appeal against such decision within forty-five days of the issuance of the order conveying the decision, before ‒</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) the Chief Executive Officer, where such orders have been passed by an officer subordinate to him; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the Authority, where such orders have been passed by the Chief Executive Officer:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided further that the Chief Executive Officer shall not participate in the meeting of the Authority where an appeal on his decision is to be decided. Chapter II Categories of posts and classification</p>
</div>
