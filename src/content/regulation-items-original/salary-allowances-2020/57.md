parent: salary-allowances-2020
number: "57"
title: "Criticism of Authority or Government"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall, in any radio broadcast, telecast through any electronic media or any document published in his own name or anonymously, pseudonymously or in the name of any other person or in any communication to the press or in any public utterance, make any statement of fact or opinion which has the effect of an adverse criticism of any current or recent policy or action of the Authority or Government.</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that nothing in this regulation shall apply to any statements made or views expressed by an employee in his official capacity or in the due performance of the duties assigned to him.</p>
</div>
