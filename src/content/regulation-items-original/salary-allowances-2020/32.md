title: "Prohibition against participation in politics and standing for election"
as_of: 2020-08-06
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall take active part in politics or in any political demonstration, or stand for election as member of a Municipal Council, District Authority or any other Local Body or any Legislative Body.</p>
</div>
