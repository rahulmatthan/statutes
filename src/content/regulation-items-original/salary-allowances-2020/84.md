<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Every employee shall be bound to observe safety guidelines as specified from time to time by the Authority in discharge of official duties and breach of guidelines shall be construed as misconduct and such employee shall be liable for disciplinary proceedings.</p>
</div>
