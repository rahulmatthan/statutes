title: "Rejection of enrolment"
chapter: "II — Resident Enrolment Process"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Authority may reject an enrolment due to there being duplicate enrolments by the resident, quality or any other technical reason.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In case of rejection due to duplicate enrolment, resident may be informed about the enrolment against which his Aadhaar number has been generated in the manner as may be specified by the Authority.</p>
</div>
