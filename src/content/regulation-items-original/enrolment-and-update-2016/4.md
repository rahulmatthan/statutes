parent: enrolment-and-update-2016
number: "4"
title: "Demographic information required for enrolment"
chapter: "II — Resident Enrolment Process"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The following demographic information shall be collected from all individuals undergoing enrolment (other than children below five years of age):</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Name</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Date of Birth</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) Gender</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) Residential Address</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The following demographic information may also additionally be collected during enrolment, at the option of the individual undergoing enrolment:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Mobile number</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Email address</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) In case of Introducer-based enrolment, the following additional information shall be collected:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Introducer name</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Introducer’s Aadhaar number</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(4) In case of Head of Family based enrolment, the following additional information shall be collected:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Name of Head of Family</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Relationship</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) Head of Family’s Aadhaar number</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) One modality of biometric information of the Head of Family</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(5) The standards of the above demographic information shall be as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(6) The demographic information shall not include race, religion, caste, tribe, ethnicity, language, record of entitlement, income or medical history of the resident.</p>
</div>
