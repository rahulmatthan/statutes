title: "Liability of Registrars, enrolling agencies and other service providers and action in case of default"
chapter: "V — Appointment Of Registrars, Enrolling Agencies And Other Service"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Registrars, enrolling agencies, and other service providers, and the supervisors, operators or any other persons or agencies employed by them shall adhere to all regulations, processes, standards, guidelines, and orders issued by the Authority from time to time, and the code of conduct provided in Schedule V.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The Authority shall monitor the enrolment activities of the Registrars, enrolling agencies and the operators, supervisors and other personnel associated with enrolment.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Without prejudice to action that may be taken under the Act, violation of any regulation, process, standard, guideline or order by any service provider or other person may result in immediate suspension of the activities of such service providers or the concerned persons, and after holding due inquiry as deemed fit by the Authority, cancellation of their credentials, codes and permissions issued to them pursuant to the Act or these regulations.</p>
</div>
