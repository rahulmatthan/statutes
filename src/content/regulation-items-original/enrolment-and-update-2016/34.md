title: "Power to issue policies, process documents, etc"
chapter: "VIII — Miscellaneous"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The Authority may issue policies, orders, processes, standards, specifications and other documents, not inconsistent with these regulations, which are required to be specified under these regulations or for which provision is necessary for the purpose of giving effect to these regulations.</p>
</div>
