title: "Rectification action"
chapter: "VI — Omission Or Deactivation Of Aadhaar Number And Identity Information"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In case of omission of Aadhaar number for reasons other than multiple Aadhaar numbers having been issued, residents shall be required to re-enrol.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In case of deactivation, a resident shall be required to update his identity information partly or fully as required, as per the guidelines or policies as may be specified by the Authority.</p>
</div>
