title: "Delivery of Aadhaar number"
chapter: "II — Resident Enrolment Process"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Aadhaar number may be communicated to residents in physical form (including letters or cards) and/ or electronic form (available for download through the Authority’s website or through SMS).</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) All agencies engaged by the Authority for printing, dispatch, and other functions related to delivery shall comply with the applicable processes.</p>
</div>
