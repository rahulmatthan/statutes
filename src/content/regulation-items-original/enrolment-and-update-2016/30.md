number: "30"
title: "Communication to the Aadhaar number holder"
chapter: "VI — Omission Or Deactivation Of Aadhaar Number And Identity Information"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) An Aadhaar number holder shall be informed of the omission or deactivation of their Aadhaar number along with reasons through SMS, registered e-mail ID, tele-calling, letter or through such means as deemed fit by the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Any complaint of an Aadhaar number holder in respect of such omission or deactivation may be addressed through the grievance redressal mechanism set up by the Authority.</p>
</div>
