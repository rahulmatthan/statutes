---
parent: sharing-of-information-2016
number: "5"
title: "Responsibility of any agency or entity other than requesting entity with respect to Aadhaar number"
chapter: "II — Restrictions On Sharing Of Identity Information"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Any individual, agency or entity which collects Aadhaar number or any document containing the Aadhaar number, shall:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) collect, store and use the Aadhaar number for a lawful purpose;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) inform the Aadhaar number holder the following details:— i. the purpose for which the information is collected; ii. whether submission of Aadhaar number or proof of Aadhaar for such purpose is mandatory or voluntary, and if mandatory, the legal provision mandating it; iii. alternatives to submission of Aadhaar number or the document containing Aadhaar number, if any;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) obtain consent of the Aadhaar number holder to the collection, storage and use of his Aadhaar number for the specified purposes.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Such individual, agency or entity shall not use the Aadhaar number for any purpose other than those specified to the Aadhaar number holder at the time of obtaining his consent.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Such individual, agency or entity shall not share the Aadhaar number with any person without the consent of the Aadhaar number holder.</p>
</div>
