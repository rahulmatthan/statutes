title: "Reimbursement of expenses"
as_of: 2016-09-12
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Special invitees to any meeting of the Authority shall be entitled to a payment of two thousand rupees for attending each meeting and the reimbursement of actual travel and subsistence expenses.</p>
</div>
