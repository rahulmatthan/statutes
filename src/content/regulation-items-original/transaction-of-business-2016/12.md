<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Any item of business or issue which requires decision of the Authority, if the Chairperson so directs, may be referred to Members by circulation of the matter as a resolution.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) A resolution shall be deemed to have been passed by the Members when such a resolution circulated in the draft, together with necessary material, if any, to all the Members to their official email ids and the resolution is approved by the majority of the Members:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that the replies of all Members shall be obtained within a period not more than three clear days.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Any resolution circulated under sub-regulation (1) and approved by the majority of such of those Members entitled to vote thereon, shall have such effect and be binding as if such resolution were decided by the majority of the members at a meeting.</p>
