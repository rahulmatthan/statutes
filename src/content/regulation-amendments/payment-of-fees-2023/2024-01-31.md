This amending instrument records 3 discrete changes to the principal regulations: a small phrase insertion into the Explanation in regulation 3, the insertion of two new sub-regulations (4) and (5) in regulation 3 (the “authentication-with-update” fee mechanism), and the insertion of a new regulation 5 on delegated acts.
