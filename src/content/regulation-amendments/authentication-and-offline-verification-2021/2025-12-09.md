This amending instrument records 15 discrete changes — four new/deleted definitions in regulation 2(1), the substitution of regulation 3A and the Chapter III title, the insertion of OVSE registration (13A) and surrender (23A) provisions, three deletions of words referring to m-Aadhaar / XML / KSA, and three substitutions to regulation 25 (penalty action against OVSEs). Each is captured verbatim above.
