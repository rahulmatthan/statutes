<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) No employee, shall:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) Ever encourage outside agencies to call on him at his residence or temporary headquarters while on tour for discussion on official matters.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) In dealing with the ecosystem partners and public for seeking any clarification on matters relating to them, resort to oral clarifications and personal discussions as far as possible and written communication shall be the normal practice.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) Use office facilities like letter heads for non-official or personal purposes.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) Ever be involved in any type of activities/favours and physical facilities so as to place himself in an embarrassing position in the discharge of his duties with integrity and fairness.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) An employee is strictly prohibited from using his official position for any personal favour for himself or his family members or his relatives or friends.</p>
</div>
