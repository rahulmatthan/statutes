<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Save as provided in sub-regulation (3) no employee shall, except with the prior approval of the Competent Authority, give evidence in connection with any enquiry conducted by any person, committee or authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Where any approval has been accorded under sub-regulation (1), no employee giving such evidence shall criticize the policy or any action of the Central Government or the State Government or the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Nothing in this regulation shall apply to any evidence given:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) at any enquiry before an authority appointed by the Central Government, State Government, Parliament or a State Legislature; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) in any judicial enquiry; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) at any departmental enquiry ordered by the Competent Authority.</p>
</div>
