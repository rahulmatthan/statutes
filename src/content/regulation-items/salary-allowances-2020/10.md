title: "Pay and allowances when cease to accrue"
chapter: "IV — Pay, Allowances And Other Benefits To Employees"
status: original
history:
  - date: 2020-08-06
    by: original
    kind: enacted
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Pay and allowances shall cease to accrue from the date an employee ceases to be in service.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In case of an employee dismissed, removed or compulsorily retired from the Authority's service, the pay and allowances shall cease from the date of his dismissal, removal or compulsory retirement. In the case of an employee who dies while in service, they shall cease from the day following that on which the death occurs.</p>
</div>
