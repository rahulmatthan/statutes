title: "Vindication of acts and character of employee"
chapter: "V — Conduct, Discipline And Appeal"
status: original
history:
  - date: 2020-08-06
    by: original
    kind: enacted
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) No employee shall, except with the prior sanction of the Competent Authority, have recourse to any Court or to the press, for the vindication of any official act which has been the subject matter of adverse criticism or an attack of a defamatory character.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Nothing in this regulation shall be deemed to prohibit an employee from vindicating his private character or any act done by him in private capacity and where any action for vindicating his private character or any act done by him in private capacity is taken, the employee shall submit a report to the Competent Authority regarding such action.</p>
</div>
