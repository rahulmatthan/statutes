<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall, except with the prior sanction of the Competent Authority, ask for or accept a contribution to or otherwise associate himself with the raising of any funds or other collections in cash or in kind in pursuance of any objective whatsoever.</p>
</div>
