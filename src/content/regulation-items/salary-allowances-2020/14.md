  - date: 2020-08-06
    by: original

<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The employees of the Authority shall be eligible for general pool residential accommodation for a period of five years from the date of notification of the establishment of the Authority, vide Directorate of Estates, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs Office Memorandum No. 11013/D/11/2009-Pol dated the 29th November 2017.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Employees of the Authority, not being allotted residential accommodation, shall be eligible for such House Rent Allowance as are admissible to the employees of the Central Government in the corresponding grade.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The Authority may create or acquire residential accommodation for its employees and determine a scheme for allotment thereof.</p>
</div>
