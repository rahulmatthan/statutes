title: "Not to address appeals, representations, petitions to outside authority or person"
chapter: "V — Conduct, Discipline And Appeal"
status: original
history:
  - date: 2020-08-06
    by: original
    kind: enacted
---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">No employee shall address any appeal, representation or petition to any outside authority or person, in respect of a matter pertaining to the employee's service in the Authority. Addressing such appeals, representations or petitions shall be deemed to be a breach of discipline.</p>
</div>
