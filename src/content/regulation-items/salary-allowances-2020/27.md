<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">All other rules and orders issued by the Central Government from time to time to govern the benefits and entitlements for the employees of the Central Government, which have not been expressly covered under these regulations, shall, mutatis mutandis, apply to the employees of the Authority.</p>
