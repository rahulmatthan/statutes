<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The employees of the Authority, other than those on deputation, shall be eligible for benefit of gratuity under Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 (39 of 1972), as amended from time to time in the event of:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) retirement;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) death;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) disablement rendering him unfit for further service as certified by the medical officer appointed by the Authority;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) resignation after completing five years of continuous service; or</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) termination of service in any other way (except by way of punishment) after completion of five years of service.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) For the purpose of sub-regulation (1), the Authority shall create Unique Identification Authority of India Gratuity Fund.</p>
</div>
