title: "Pay and allowances when not payable for part of a month"
chapter: "IV — Pay, Allowances And Other Benefits To Employees"

  - date: 2020-08-06

<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Pay and allowances shall not be payable for a part of a month to an employee who leaves or discontinues his service without due notice during a month, unless such notice has been waived by the Competent Authority.</p>
</div>
