<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Other service providers may be appointed or engaged by the Authority from time to time for discharging any function related to the 40[***] enrolment process or updating of information.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) All such service providers shall adhere to the regulations, processes, standards, guidelines, and orders issued by the Authority from time to time, and the code of conduct provided in Schedule V of these regulations, as applicable.</p>
