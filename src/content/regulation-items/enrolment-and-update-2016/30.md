<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="enrolment-and-update-2016/2025-08-22" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2025-08-22" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2025-08-22" data-target="regulation 30, sub-regulation (1), after the second proviso">(1) An Aadhaar number holder shall be informed of the omission or deactivation of their Aadhaar number along with reasons through SMS, registered e-mail ID, tele-calling, letter or through such means as deemed fit by the Authority: [Provided that the same shall not be required in case of deactivation of Aadhaar number of a child for the want of mandatory biometric information update.</span></p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided further that the details of deactivation of Aadhaar number of a child for want of mandatory biometric update shall be available on the UIDAI portal from which the Aadhaar number holder can verify his/ her details.] [Provided further that the same shall not be required in case of deactivation of Aadhaar number of a deceased person under clause (ea) of sub-Regulation (1) of regulation 28.]</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Any complaint of an Aadhaar number holder in respect of such omission or deactivation may be addressed through the grievance redressal mechanism set up by the Authority.</p>
