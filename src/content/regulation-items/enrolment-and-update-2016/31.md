<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) In case of omission of Aadhaar number for reasons other than multiple Aadhaar numbers having been issued, <span class="amend" data-id="p12.58" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="resident" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">the individual concerned</span> shall be required to re-enrol.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In case of deactivation, <span class="amend" data-id="p12.59" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="a resident" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">the Aadhaar number holder</span> shall be required to update his identity information partly or fully as required, as per the guidelines or policies as may be specified by the Authority.</p>
