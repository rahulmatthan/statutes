  <p class="block clause">(a) Any document listed in the above tabular statement shall be acceptable only if it meets the following conditions, namely:—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) It is currently valid;</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) The person in respect of whom such document is issued is entitled for the same;</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) The information contained in the document is verifiable from the source; and</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) The authority issuing the document has not made any declaration that such document is not a proof of identity, address, date of birth or relationship for which such a document is presented.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) Criteria for acceptability of the documents submitted to evidence identity, address, date of birth and relationship:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) A document is accepted as Proof of Identity (PoI) document only if it contains at least the name and photograph of the individual.</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) PoI, PoA, PoR and PDB documents submitted for enrolment must be issued in the name of the individual seeking enrolment.</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) A document is accepted as Proof of Relationship (PoR) document only if it contains at least the name of individual and name of Head of Family (HoF).</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) Name of the individual as mentioned in the supporting document, will be replicated as such in the individual’s Aadhaar. No additional information will be considered for inclusion in the name.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(v) Name and date of birth of the individual across all the documents submitted as PoI, PoR and PDB must be uniform.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) Head of Family (HoF) based enrolment is mandatory for children below five years of age except for children in Child Care Institutions and foreign nationals.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) Criteria for Head of Family and Proof of Relationship based enrolment:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) HoF must have a valid Aadhaar before performing HoF based enrolment.</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Any one of the parents or the legal guardian can be HoF.</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) Aadhaar number of both the parents is required for HoF based enrolment. In case the child is living with only one parent or living with a legal guardian, the Aadhaar number of only such parent or legal guardian may be given.</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) Biometric authentication by one of the parents or the legal guardian is mandatory.</p>
  <p class="block clause">(v) The address mentioned in the Aadhaar of HoF will be used for the address in the Aadhaar of the child.</p>
