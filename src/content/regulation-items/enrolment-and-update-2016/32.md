<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Authority shall set up a contact centre to act as a central point of contact for resolution of queries and grievances of <span class="amend" data-id="p13.60" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol and Aadhaar number holders</span>, accessible to <span class="amend" data-id="p13.60" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol and Aadhaar number holders</span> through toll free number(s) and/ or e-mail, as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The contact centre shall:</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) Provide a mechanism to log queries or grievances and provide <span class="amend" data-id="p13.60" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol and Aadhaar number holders</span> with a unique reference number for further tracking till closure of the matter;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) Provide regional language support to the extent possible;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) Ensure safety of any information received from <span class="amend" data-id="p13.60" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol and Aadhaar number holders</span> in relation to their identity information;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) Comply with the procedures and processes as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) <span class="amend" data-id="p13.60" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol and Aadhaar number holders</span> may also raise grievances by visiting the regional offices of the Authority or through any other officers or channels as may be specified by the Authority.</p>
</div>
