<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Authority may reject an enrolment due to there being duplicate enrolments by the <span class="amend" data-id="p5.26" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="resident" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">Aadhaar number holder</span>, quality or anyother technical reason.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) In case of rejection due to duplicate enrolment, <span class="amend" data-id="p5.26" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="resident" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">Aadhaar number holder</span> may be informed about the enrolment against which his Aadhaar number has been generated in the manner as may be specified by the Authority.</p>
