<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The following biometric information shall be collected from all individuals undergoing enrolment (other than children below five years of age):</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) Facial image;</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) All ten fingerprints; and</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) Scans of both irises.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The standards for collecting the biometric information shall be as specified by the Authority for this purpose.</p>
</div>
