<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">Any Central or State department or agency which requires an individual to undergo authentication or furnish proof of possession of Aadhaar number as a condition for receipt of any subsidy, benefit or service pursuant to Section 7 of the Act, shall ensure enrolment of its beneficiaries who are yet to be enrolled <span class="amend" data-id="p5.20" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2017-07-07" data-by="unknown" data-by-text="Notification No. 13012/79/2017/Legal-UIDAI" data-note="Inserted by amendment regulations">or update their Aadhaar details</span>, through appropriate measures, including co-ordination with Registrars and setting up enrolment centres at convenient locations or providing enrolment facilities by becoming a Registrar itself. [12A. <span class="amend" data-id="p5.22" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2017-07-11" data-by="unknown" data-by-text="Notification No. 13012/79/2017/Legal-UIDAI" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">Entities</span> requiring Aadhaar as condition for fulfillment of any obligation, etc.- The Authority may require any Central or State department <span class="amend" data-id="p5.23" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2017-07-11" data-by="unknown" data-by-text="Notification No. 13012/79/2017/Legal-UIDAI" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">or agency or any Scheduled Bank or any other entity</span> which requires an individual to undergo authentication or furnish proof of possession of Aadhaar number as a condition for receipt of any subsidy, benefit, service or fulfillment of any obligation pursuant to <span class="amend" data-id="p5.24" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2017-07-11" data-by="unknown" data-by-text="Notification No. 13012/79/2017/Legal-UIDAI" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">any Act</span> or Rule or Regulation or order made thereunder, to ensure enrolment of such individual who is yet to be enrolled or update their Aadhaar details, by setting up enrolment centers at their premises.] [GENERATION OF AADHAAR NUMBERS AND THEIR DELIVERY</p></div>
