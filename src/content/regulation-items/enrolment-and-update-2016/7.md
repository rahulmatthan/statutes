<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Registrars appointed by the Authority shall carry out the enrolment of <span class="amend" data-id="p3.13" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol</span> by themselves or through enrolling agencies contracted/appointed by them.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The enrolling agencies shall set up enrolment centres for enrolment of <span class="amend" data-id="p3.13" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol</span> as well as for correction or update of <span class="amend" data-id="p3.14" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents’ information" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">information of Aadhaar number holders</span>. The opening of enrolment centres, the services offered and other guidelines for their operations shall be in accordance with the procedure as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Enrolment shall be carried out at permanent enrolment centres and through temporary centres set up for this purpose.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(4) Enrolment shall be carried out by the operators, verifiers, supervisors and other authorized personnel at the enrolment centres.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(5) Registrars shall make reasonable efforts to enrol <span class="amend" data-id="p3.13" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-01-27" data-by="enrolment-and-update-2016/2024-01-27" data-by-text="Notification No. HQ-16016/1/2023-EU-I-" data-before="residents" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">individuals seeking to enrol</span> who do not possess any of the prescribed supporting documents, through alternate modes of enrolment specified in regulation 10.</p>
</div>
