<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">The following information in respect of a child below the age of five years who is undergoing enrolment shall be collected, namely:—</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) Name;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) Date of birth;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) Gender;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) Facial image;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) Aadhaar number of parents or the guardian, along with the details of the relationship with the child;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(f) Biometric information of either parent or the guardian, along with his address:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that if the Aadhaar number of the parents or guardian is not available, the reasons for the same shall be recorded:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided further that the identity of the parent or guardian referred to in clause (f) shall be established through biometric-based authentication.</p></div>
