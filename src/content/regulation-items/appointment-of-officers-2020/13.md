<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The posts which are to be filled up on contract basis shall be filled for a specified period on such terms and conditions and qualification criteria as specified by the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) The contractual appointment shall be made only in the exigency of work to meet the short term requirements.</p>
</div>
