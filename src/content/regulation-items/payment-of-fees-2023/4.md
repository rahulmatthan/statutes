<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The fee chargeable under regulation 3 shall be payable within a period of thirty days from the date of issuance of an invoice by the Authority in this behalf.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Any delay in payment of the fee chargeable under regulation 3, beyond expiry of the period referred to in sub-regulation (1), shall attract interest at the rate of 1.5per cent. per month, compounded monthly.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) Any KUA, sub-KUA, AUA or sub-AUA referred to in regulation 3, who discontinues the use of the Authentication facility referred to therein shall intimate such discontinuation to the Authority:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that the fee specified in regulation 3 shall continue to be chargeable till the surrender of access to the Authentication facility by the KUA, sub-KUA, AUA or sub-AUA, as the case may be, and the deactivation of such access.</p>
</div>
