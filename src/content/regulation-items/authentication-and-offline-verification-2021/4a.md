  - date: 2021-11-08

---
<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) Authority shall provide an alternate identification number mapped with Aadhaar number for the purpose of authentication.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Aadhaar number holder may generate or retrieve his/her VID through UIDAI website, SMS, mobile application, eAadhaar download and any other means as provided by Authority from time to time.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) The Aadhaar number holder may use VID in lieu of Aadhaar number for online authentication or e-KYC.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(4) No entity shall store Virtual ID in its system.</p>
</div>
