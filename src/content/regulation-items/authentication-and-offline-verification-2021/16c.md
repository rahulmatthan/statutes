  <p class="block subsection"><span class="amend amend-block" data-id="authentication-and-offline-verification-2021/2025-12-09" data-kind="omitted" data-date="2025-12-09" data-by="authentication-and-offline-verification-2021/2025-12-09" data-target="regulation 16C, sub-regulation (1)" data-before="m-Aadhaar or" data-note="The words “m-Aadhaar or” and the word “(XML)” are both deleted from sub-regulation (1).">(1) No Offline Verification Seeking Entity shall accept Aadhaar number, in physical or electronic form (without authentication), as a proof of identity for a lawful purpose, without first verifying the digital signature of the Authority as provided in the Aadhaar secure QR Code on Aadhaar Letter or e-Aadhaar or 29[Omitted] Aadhaar Paperless Offline e-KYC 30[Omitted], as the case may be.</span></p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) No requesting entity shall accept Aadhaar number as a proof of identity of the Aadhaar number holder, in electronic form by way of authentication, unless it is for a lawful purpose which is in conformity with the relevant provisions of the Act and only with the informed consent of the Aadhaar number holder and in a manner as provided in these Aadhaar (Authentication and Offline Verification) Regulations, 2021.</p></div>
