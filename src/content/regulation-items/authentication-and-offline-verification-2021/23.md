<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) A <span class="amend" data-id="p21.32" data-kind="substituted" data-date="2024-12-04" data-by="unknown" data-note="Substituted by amendment regulations">requesting entity</span> or ASA, appointed under these regulations, desirous of surrendering the access to the authentication facility granted by Authority, may make a request for such surrender to the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) While disposing such surrender request under these regulations, the Authority may require the requesting entity or ASA to satisfy the Authority about any matter necessary for smooth discontinuance or termination of services, including -</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) the arrangements made by the requesting entity for maintenance and preservation of authentication logs and other documents in accordance with these regulations and procedures as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the arrangements made by the requesting entity for making authentication record available to the respective Aadhaar number holder on such request;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) records of redressal of grievances, if any;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) settlement of accounts with the Authority, if any;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(e) in case of surrender by ASAs, the ASA, prior to the surrender of its access, shall ensure that its associated requesting entities are given adequate time to migrate to other ASAs in operation. [23A. Surrender of the access to offline verification facility by OVSE.–</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(1) An OVSE registered under these regulations, desirous of surrendering the access to the offline verification facility granted by Authority, may make a request for such surrender to the Authority.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) While disposing such surrender request under these regulations, the Authority may require the OVSE to satisfy the Authority about any matter necessary for smooth discontinuance or termination of services, including –</p>
  <p class="block clause">(a) the arrangements made by the OVSE for maintenance and preservation of verification logs and other documents in accordance with these regulations and procedures as may be specified by the Authority for this purpose;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(b) the arrangements made by the OVSE for making verification record available to the respective Aadhaar number holder on such request;</p>
  <p class="block clause">(c) records of redressal of grievances, if any; and</p>
  <p class="block clause">(d) settlement of accounts with the Authority, if any.”</p>
</div>
