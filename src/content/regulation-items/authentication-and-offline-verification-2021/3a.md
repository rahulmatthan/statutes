<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">1. There shall be following types of Offline Verification services provided by the Authority, namely-</p>
  <p class="block clause">(i) QR Code verification,</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii) Aadhaar Paperless Offline e-KYC verification,</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(ii)(a) Aadhaar Verifiable Credential verification,</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iii) e-Aadhaar verification,</p>
  <p class="block subclause">(iv) Offline Paper based verification, and</p>
  <p class="block clause">(v) Any other type of Offline verification introduced by the Authority from time to time. Offline Verification as above may be carried out by the entity <span class="amend" data-id="p4.13" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2025-12-09" data-by="authentication-and-offline-verification-2021/2025-12-09" data-note="Inserted by amendment regulations">with or without offline face verification} as per the specifications given by the Authority from time to time. </span> 2. The Authority shall provide various means to <span class="amend" data-id="p5.14" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2025-12-09" data-by="authentication-and-offline-verification-2021/2025-12-09" data-note="Inserted by amendment regulations">scan QR code and</span> download QR Code, or e-Aadhaar or Aadhaar Paperless Offline e-KYC <span class="amend" data-id="p5.15" data-kind="inserted" data-date="2025-12-09" data-by="authentication-and-offline-verification-2021/2025-12-09" data-note="Inserted by amendment regulations">or Aadhaar Verifiable Credential</span> through [Aadhaar] application or other means.</p>
</div>
