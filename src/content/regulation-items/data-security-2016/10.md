<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">In order to clarify any matter pertaining to application or interpretation of these regulations, or to remove any difficulties in implementation of these regulations, the Authority shall have the power to issue clarifications and guidelines in the form of circulars which shall have effect of these regulations. ***</p>
