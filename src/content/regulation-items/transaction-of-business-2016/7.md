<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block subsection">(1) The Chief Executive Officer shall be responsible for preparation of the agenda for each meeting of the Authority and circulation thereof to all the Members of the Authority at least a period of seven days before the date of each meeting:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided that the Chief Executive Officer shall prepare the agenda of meeting in consultation with the Chairperson:</p>
  <p class="block proviso">Provided further that Chief Executive Officer may, in consultation with the Chairperson, amend or delete or add new item in the Agenda of business subsequent to its issue.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(2) Any business not included in the agenda shall not be transacted at a meeting of the Authority except with the permission of the Chairperson or other Member presiding over the meeting.</p>
  <p class="block subsection">(3) With the permission of the Chairperson or other Member presiding over the meeting, urgent questions may be added to the Agenda at any time before the conclusion of the meeting, or any item on the Agenda may be deleted or carried over to a subsequent meeting.</p>
</div>
