<div class="statute-body">
  <p class="block lead">In the event the identity information of an Aadhaar number holder has been shared or published in a manner contrary to the provisions of the Act or regulations, the Aadhaar number holder may raise queries and grievances in accordance with the regulation 32 of the Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016.</p>
</div>
